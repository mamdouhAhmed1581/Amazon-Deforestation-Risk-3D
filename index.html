🛠 Project Workflow

1. The Core Problem

Predicting deforestation risk using multidimensional spatial and environmental variables to act proactively.

2. Data Foundation

Target dataset: forest_data_clean.json comprising 150,000 spatial coordinates, each containing geospatial traits, risk indices, and topographical elevation.

3. The Risk Engine

Weighted parameter algorithm simulating environmental threat vectors:

Risk = 0.30(Loss) + 0.20(Roads)
+ 0.15(Pop.) + 0.15(Fire)
+ 0.10(Slope) + 0.10(Elev)

4. Spatial Validation

Cross-referencing active risk zones against historical fire registries and hotspot clusters utilizing DBSCAN. Performance: ROC AUC = 0.82

5. Final Conclusions

High-risk areas are predominantly concentrated along developing road networks and agricultural expansion zones spanning the Southeastern Amazon.
