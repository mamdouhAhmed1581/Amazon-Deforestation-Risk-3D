🌐 Study Area & GeoAI Framework

This project analyzes 150,000 unique spatial points within the Amazon Basin, focusing on Rondônia, Brazil. The GABBAR engine predicts future degradation by correlating satellite multi-spectral bands with human infrastructure expansion.

Technical Path: Data Acquisition (PRODES/VIIRS) → U-Net Binary Segmentation → Temporal Risk Estimation.

📊 Spatial Validation & AUC Score

Validation results demonstrate high predictive reliability with a ROC AUC score of 0.82. The model successfully clusters fire events (Hotspots) using DBSCAN algorithms.

Risk(x,y,t) = 0.3(Loss) + 0.2(Dist) + 0.15(Fire) + 0.15(Pop) + 0.1(Slope) + 0.1(Elev)
