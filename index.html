🌍 STUDY AREA & SCOPE

Located in Rondônia, Brazil, our research focuses on 150,000 unique spatial points within the "Arc of Deforestation." The model analyzes multi-temporal satellite data to identify degradation patterns.

Primary Objective: Real-time mapping and future risk prediction of forest loss using Neural-Prophet and CNN architectures.

🧠 NEURAL ENGINE & VALIDATION

Performance metrics indicate high predictive stability with an AUC score of 0.82. The system correlates historical burning events with spatial features to forecast hotspots.

Risk Index = Σ [0.30(Loss) + 0.20(Roads) + 0.15(Fire) + 0.15(Pop) + 0.10(Slope) + 0.10(Elev)]
